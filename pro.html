Build your apps faster and smarter with premium AI access and exclusive Pro modes

Denjin Pro doesn't just give you API credits to use the best AI models. It unlocks powerful Pro modes that lets you use AI more efficiently and effectively on large projects.

AI credits
You will receive 300 credits that gives you access to cutting-edge models like GPT 4.1, Gemini 2.5 Pro, and Claude Sonnet 4. Denjin gives you the choice to use your favorite models.

Have questions about credits? See our FAQ section for detailed information.

Turbo Edits
Turbo Edits optimizes your AI credits by offloading routine code changes from the state-of-the-art model to a faster, more affordable model specialized in editing code.
Smart Context
Smart Context enhances your AI interactions by automatically including the most relevant files from your codebase in the chat context. It uses a secondary, lower-cost AI model to analyze your request and select the key files needed. This mode makes interactions up to 5x more credit-efficient and is particularly useful for large projects.
